I am a fashion designer who graduated from IAAD, the Institute of Applied Art, in Italy.
My academic path has been a diverse experience,
combining studies in International Business with fashion design.
This eclectic education has broadened my skills, making me a complete and versatile figure.
Passionate about crafts and upcycling in fashion,
I love experimenting and embracing new challenges.
I have long been committed to sewing a unique and delicate universe,
creating handcrafted signature pieces
with a rare and artistic approach to fashion.
My unfettered creativity is balanced by a precision and schematicism
evident in my designs, creating a unique and recognisable identity.
With a personality that mixes boldness and rigour,
I strive to transform innovative ideas into tangible realities,
making each design an authentic expression of my style and vision.
My ultimate desire is to make the people who wear my creations feel unique,
which is why the garments are carefully made one at a time in house and are available in limited quantities.
